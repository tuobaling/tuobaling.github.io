Notion Task Management

26 Mar 2026 • 1 min read

Notion Task Management inspired by the Card Box (Zettelkasten) method and C.O.D.E workflow.

Introduce

A powerful, project-based task management system inspired by the Card Box (Zettelkasten) method and the C.O.D.E workflow.

✝️ I want it →

Features

  • Project-Based Organization — Group tasks by project for clear context
  • Card Box Concept — Break down complex projects into atomic, linkable cards
  • C.O.D.E Workflow — Follow the Capture → Organize → Distill → Express cycle
  • Upper Card Tracking — Link cards to parent tasks for hierarchical view (Pro feature)

How to Use

  1. Capture — Add new ideas, tasks, or notes as cards
  2. Organize — Assign cards to projects and link related cards
  3. Distill — Break big tasks into smaller actionable cards
  4. Express — Complete and track your progress

💡 Pro Tip: Use the “Net Card” property to create a hierarchy — each card can reference, giving you a bird’s-eye view of your project structure.
